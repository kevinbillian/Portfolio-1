    Internship Story

    Bill started his career with Deloitte as a Cyber Risk Advisory Intern. He was involved in Identity Access Management (IAM) Implementation project for one of the biggest telecommunication company in Asia-Pacific.

    He worked with project team performing risk consultancy, server deployment (architecture, software, cryptographic key and proxy), software development,and its various testing and project management.

    In addition, he wrote installation guide, operation guide and test results report for clients. Also, he assisted director and managers by contributing in several documentations such as communication matrix, traceability matrix and project schedule.

    He is very grateful for the internship opportunity. Not only he worked with and learn from creative and supportive cyber security professionals in Deloitte, but also he was introduced and exposed to a field that quite frankly has become his passion since.

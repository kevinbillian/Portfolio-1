About Me

Bill is a third year Computer Science student at Lassonde School of Engineering,York University.


He is a lifelong learner and is passionate about cyber security. Particularly in encouraging people about the importance and awareness of cyber security in real world.


He started his career as a Cyber Risk Advisory - Intern at Deloitte. During this time, he assisted in various areas such as Risk Assessment, Identity Access Management Implementation, and Project Management. Projects that he was involved in varies from financial industry (private), to telecommunication industry (public).


Currently, he is pursuing Security+ and CEH certification. He is planning to obtain CISSP and CISA certifications in the near future.


During his spare time, he loves to write articles about cybersecurity, personal development and mental health in open source medias.

This site has been designed to documents and profiles my projects, learning, internship and volunteer experiences that I have been doing with a diverse community of professionals, and clientele.


I intend for this portfolio to be a living record of my career journey and growth. As I develop and gain new skills and experience, so will this portfolio.


I also share some resources that helped me learning about topics such as: Programming, Computer Security, Work Life Balance and Personal Development.


For a more comprehensive view of my work and experience, please consult my resume and projects section.


Contact me for collaboration or other opportunities.

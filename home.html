About Me

Bill is a lifelong learner and is passionate about cyber security. Particularly in encouraging people about the importance and awareness of cyber security in real world.


He started his career as a Cyber Risk Advisory - Intern at Deloitte. During this time, he assisted in various areas such as Risk Assessment, Identity Access Management Implementation, and Project Management. Projects that he was involved in varies from financial industry (private), to telecommunication industry (public).


Currently, he is pursuing Security+ and CEH certification. He is planning to obtain CISSP and CISA certifications in the near future.


During his spare time, he loves to write articles about cybersecurity, personal development and mental health in open source medias.

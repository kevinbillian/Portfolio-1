About Me

Bill is a third year Computer Science student at Lassonde School of Engineering,York University. He is a lifelong learner and is passionate about cyber security, particularly in encouraging people about importance and awareness of cyber security in real world.

He started his career as an Cyber Risk Advisory - Intern at Deloitte. During this time, he assisted in various areas such as risk assessment, secured identity access management implementation, and project management. Projects that he is involved in range from financial institution (private), and telecommunication (public).

Currently pursuing Security+ certification and is planning to pursue CISSP and CISA certifications in the near future.

He also loves to write articles about cybersecurity, personal development and mental health in open source medias.
